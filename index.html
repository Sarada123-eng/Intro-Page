About Warren Buffett

Warren Buffett, known as the "Oracle of Omaha," is one of the most successful investors of all time.Warren Edward Buffett was born on August 30, 1930, in Omaha, Nebraska, USA.He attended the University of Pennsylvania and later transferred to the University of Nebraska. Buffett completed his graduate studies at Columbia Business School, where he was influenced by Benjamin Graham, the "father of value investing. Buffett began investing in and eventually took control of a struggling textile company called Berkshire Hathaway in the 1960s. He transformed it into a holding company for investments in a diverse range of industries, including insurance, energy, transportation, and consumer goods. As the chairman and CEO of Berkshire Hathaway, his leadership has turned the company into a global conglomerate. Buffett is celebrated for his value investing philosophy and philanthropy.
